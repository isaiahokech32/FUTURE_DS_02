Insights and Business Recommendations
Key Insights

    Overall campaign performance was good, with strong reach, engagement, and positive returns across campaigns.

    Ad types performed almost similarly, indicating no significant performance gaps in engagement or cost efficiency.

    Campaign categories showed only slight differences, suggesting relatively balanced execution across campaigns.

    No negative ROI was observed, confirming that all campaigns were profitable.

    Audience and platform performance varied moderately but did not significantly impact overall profitability.

    Engagement trends over time remained stable, with minor fluctuations.

Business Recommendations

    Maintain current campaign strategy, as overall performance and profitability are satisfactory.

    Continue monitoring ad types, but no immediate reallocation is required due to similar performance levels.

    Apply incremental optimizations rather than major structural changes to campaigns.

    Sustain investment across platforms and audience segments, given the absence of underperforming areas.

    Use periodic performance reviews to ensure continued positive ROI.
